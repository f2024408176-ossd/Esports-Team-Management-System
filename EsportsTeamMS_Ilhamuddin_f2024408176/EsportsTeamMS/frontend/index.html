Core Features

Everything You Need to Dominate

Powerful tools designed specifically for esports organizations of all sizes.

🎮

Team Management

Create and manage multiple esports teams, assign coaches, and track team details in real time.

👤

Player Management

Register players, maintain gamer profiles, assign roles, and monitor individual performance stats.

🏆

Tournament Tracking

Add tournaments, set prize pools, track dates, and monitor your team's tournament journey.

⚔️

Match Scheduling

Schedule upcoming matches, record results, view full match history, and analyze win rates.

📅

Training Sessions

Plan and track practice schedules, focus areas, durations, and team training attendance.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Visual insights on team performance, win/loss ratios, player stats, and tournament progress.

🔐

Secure Auth

JWT-based authentication with role-based access control for managers, coaches, and players.

🔍

Search & Filter

Quickly search players, filter matches by result, and find tournaments by date or location.

☁️

Cloud Deployed

Backend on Railway, frontend on Vercel, and database hosted on Supabase PostgreSQL.
